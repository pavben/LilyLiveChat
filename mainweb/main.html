What is LilyLiveChat?

LilyLiveChat is a customer live chat service for your website.

Can I see a demo?

Absolutely! You can see LilyLiveChat from a customer perspective or take 2 clicks to create your account and experience being a LilyLiveChat site admin or operator.

Beta Notice

LilyLiveChat is in beta for another week or two. While in beta, we'll be doing more updates than usual and without notice. As a result, running in production is only recommended for low/medium-traffic sites (less than 5,000 visits per day). After beta, which is a few weeks from now, we'll be doing updates when none of your operators are online, so you'll be waking up to new improvements with no interruptions!

Pricing

The details are still being determined, but this is what you can expect:

  • A single-operator small business plan will be around $21/month.
  • There will be a limited free starter plan for new companies with minimal traffic.
  • A three or four-operator plan will be between $50 an $70 per month.
  • Plans for larger companies will arrive a couple of weeks following the end of beta.
  • All plans will include SSL security and show at least basic customer details. Plans over $40 will offer real-time customer monitoring.
  • For actively using LilyLiveChat before the beta period is over, you are eligible for special offers of as much as 20% off the advertised prices for a whole year! To get the offer, make sure to put in your e-mail in the Admin E-mail field of your Admin Panel.

Ok, I want it! What do I do?

When you create your account, you will be given the link to your personal Admin Panel. From there, you'll be able to add operators and connect your website to LilyLiveChat. That's all there is to it!
