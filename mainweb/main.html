What is LilyLiveChat?

LilyLiveChat is a cloud-managed customer live chat service for your website.

Open Beta Notice

LilyLiveChat is in beta. Feel free to play around with it, but do not rely on it until we say so! This is because we'll be pushing lots of updates throughout the beta phase and would like to avoid the cost of updating a production environment.

Ok, I want it! What do I do?

When you create your account, you will be given the link to your personal Admin Panel. From there, you'll be able to add operators and connect your website to LilyLiveChat. That's all there is to it!
