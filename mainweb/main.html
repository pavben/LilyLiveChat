What is LilyLiveChat?

LilyLiveChat is a customer live chat service for your website.

Can I see a demo?

Absolutely! You can see LilyLiveChat from a customer perspective by chatting with LilyLiveChat Support* or take 2 clicks to create your account and experience being a LilyLiveChat site admin or operator.

* LilyLiveChat Support is here to answer your questions and play the role of an operator so that you can see what your customers will see. Have fun with it!

Pricing

The details are still in the works, but this is what you can expect:

  • A single-operator small business plan will be around $21/month.
  • A three or four-operator plan will be between $50 an $70 per month.
  • Plans with more operators will be made available in a few weeks.
  • There will be a limited free starter plan for sites with minimal traffic.
  • All plans will include SSL security and show at least basic customer details. Plans with more than one operator will also feature real-time customer monitoring.

Until the pricing is finalized, you'll be on our Unlimited Everything plan.

Ok, I want it! What do I do?

When you create your account, you will be given the link to your personal Admin Panel. From there, you'll be able to add operators and connect your website to LilyLiveChat. That's all there is to it!
